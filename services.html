About

Welcome to Smith and Shin, CPAs, LLC. We provide tax services to individuals and businesses in various industries including real estate, manufacturing, and professional services. We have experience preparing corporation, partnership, LLCs, trusts, and individual tax returns.

Our Team

Brandon Smith

Co-Owner CPA

CPA, MBA has almost twenty years of public accounting experience mostly with a Big Four national CPA firm before joining Smith and Shin, LLC. He provides consulting, tax planning, and tax preparation for over three hundred clients in various industries including real estate, service, and manufacturing both nationwide and locally. Brandon is certified in both North and South Carolina, and is a member of the American Institute of Certified Public Accountants. He earned a B.S. degree in financial accounting, cum laude, with a distinction in accounting, from the University of North Carolina at Asheville and a Masters in Business Administation from Appalachian State University. He currently serves as the Treasurer for the Upstate Carolina's Real Estate Investors Association.
