What We Do

Our Services

Comprehensive tax and accounting solutions tailored to your needs.

Individual Tax Preparation

We prepare federal and state tax returns for individuals at all income levels. Whether you have straightforward W-2 income or complex investment portfolios, rental properties, and multiple income streams, we ensure every deduction and credit is captured accurately.

Business Tax Preparation

We prepare tax returns for corporations, partnerships, LLCs, and trusts. Our team has deep experience across industries including real estate, manufacturing, and professional services, both locally in South Carolina and nationwide.

Tax Planning & Strategy

Proactive tax planning is the key to minimizing your tax liability. We work with you throughout the year to evaluate your financial situation, identify opportunities, and implement strategies that keep more money in your pocket.

Business Consulting

From entity selection and formation to ongoing financial guidance, we help business owners make informed decisions. Our consulting services cover structuring, compliance, and growth strategies tailored to your industry.

Real Estate Taxation

With extensive experience in real estate tax matters, we handle the complexities of property transactions, 1031 exchanges, depreciation strategies, and investor tax returns. Our team understands the unique challenges real estate professionals face.

IRS Representation

If you receive correspondence from the IRS or state tax authority, we can represent you and work to resolve the matter efficiently. We handle audits, notices, and disputes so you don't have to navigate them alone.
